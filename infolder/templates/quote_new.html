What is a Stock Symbol?

A stock symbol (ticker) is a unique series of letters assigned to a security for trading purposes. For example, Apple Inc. trades under the symbol AAPL, while Microsoft Corporation uses MSFT.

Pro Tip: Most US companies have 1-4 letter symbols. You can find a company's ticker symbol by searching on financial websites like Yahoo Finance or Google Finance.
